# Ardum Demo Video — Script (75 seconds)

## Scene 1: The Thesis (0-12s)

**VO:** Most travel products start with inventory. "Where do you want to go?"

**VO:** Ardum starts with intention. "What are you trying to make space for?"

**Visual:** Dark screen, serif text fades in: "the shape of your practice." Subtle jungle image in background.

---

## Scene 2: The Journey (12-30s)

**VO:** You tell Mira what you need. She clarifies — energy, budget, solitude or cohort.

**VO:** She recommends one retreat. Not a list — one.

**Visual:** Screenshot of the Ardum homepage, the intention field. Then the recommendation card with the Sidemen Restoration Retreat.

---

## Scene 3: The Grant (30-45s)

**VO:** When you're ready, you grant authority. Not a checkout — a grant.

**VO:** Mira handles the rest. Magic login, Particle Universal Account, cross-chain deposit to escrow. You never see a wallet, a chain name, or pay gas.

**Visual:** The grant ceremony — the confirm amount card. Then the "Securing your place…" status.

---

## Scene 4: Agent-Driven Booking (45-62s)

**VO:** But here's the bigger story. Any AI agent can use Ardum's API to book retreats autonomously.

**VO:** Capture intention. Clarify. Recommend. Hold. Execute on-chain deposit. Sign attestation. Booked.

**Visual:** Terminal-style output showing the agent-book.ts script running. Key lines highlighted.

---

## Scene 5: On-Chain Proof (62-70s)

**VO:** This is real. One USDC deposited to escrow on Arbitrum Sepolia. Verified on-chain.

**Visual:** Arbiscan transaction page screenshot. Block 288972600. Escrow balance: 1.0 USDC.

---

## Scene 6: The TAM (70-75s)

**VO:** Ardum is booking infrastructure for the agent economy. Any agent. Any user. Zero crypto friction.

**Visual:** Ardum logo, "mudra reversed" tagline. Fade to black.

---

## Audio notes

- Calm, confident voice — not salesy
- Background music: ambient, minimal, builds slightly at the agent booking section
- Pace: unhurried, let the visuals breathe
- Total: ~75 seconds
